Off-Score Project

A music album that you can interact with, contains 5 song/game tracks. (2020)

The Off-Score Project is a music album you can interact with.

In Off-Score, each track is both a SONG written and performed by YenTing Lo and a GAME developed by Vanja Mrgan and Ferran Bertomeu Castells.

From a piece of paper striving to survive in the society of a copy machine, to a hunter of elephants that struggles with regrettable memories, these songs and lyrics will bring you to worlds of fantasy and secrets. Only by playing through the interactive tracks will you unveil the stories behind the music.

The first two interactive tracks Copy Machine and Elephants Remember
are now available on itch.io. The rest three tracks will follow up soon, so make sure to keep an eye and follow us on these places below:
